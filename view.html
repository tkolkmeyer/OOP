4 pillars of object oriented programming

  • encapsulation
  • abstraction
  • inheritance
  • polymorphism

encapsulation

Encapsulation is the bundling of data and the restricting of direct access to an object's components

abstraction

Abstraction is the concept of showing only essential attributes and hiding unnecessary information from the user.

inheritance

Inheritance allows actions to be done on a mass scale, as the items inherit properties from a base model.

polymorphism

Polymorphism is when a single action is performed in different ways.
